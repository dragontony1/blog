Welcome!

I am currently a senior student(12th grade) at Korea Minjok Leadership Academy, a boarding school located in Hoengseong, South Korea. My area of interest includes languages(especially Swedish, Danish, Norwegian, Icelandic and Czech), linguistics(especially phonology), computer programming, railways&trains(especially those of Korean and Japanese), geography, and many more.

This website is dedicated mostly to my daily personal thoughts, ranging from useless short gibberish to long diaries. But time to time I also write about my traveling experiences: uploading photos and sharing what I saw, heard, and felt during the trip.

On the top left corner of this webpage, you will see several categories. Click on the links and you'll find a list of entries. Thank you for visiting this website, and I hope you'd enjoy.
